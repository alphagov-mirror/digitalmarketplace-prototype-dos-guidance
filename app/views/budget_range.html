Suppliers will find it helpful if you can provide a breakdown of costs so they can understand the size and scale of the project. Using a budget range is best practice, eg £750,000 to £1,000,000

The figures quoted should be excluding VAT and any travel and subsistence.

You must have budget approval before you publish your requirements.
